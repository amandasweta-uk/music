about me

Hey there,

Based in Birmingham, I'm a freelance sound designer and music composer specializing in creating atmospheric and dark ambient soundtracks for multimedia. I focus on building dystopian soundscapes, adding sound effects (SFX), and foley in post-production, and I’m skilled in audio editing for creative projects.

With a strong understanding of music composition enhanced by my background as a musician, I would love to assist in production processes and bring value to your projects—whether it’s through creating innovative soundscapes or supporting smooth workflows.
